About Smart Job Tracker

What is Smart Job Tracker?

Smart Job Tracker is a comprehensive web application designed to help job seekers manage their job search process efficiently. Built with modern technologies, it offers powerful features to track applications, analyze resumes, and improve job search strategies.

Key Features

Job Application Tracker

Keep all your job applications organized in one place. Track company details, job roles, salaries, and application status.

Resume Analyzer

Upload your resume and get AI-powered analysis. Detect skills, check ATS compatibility, and get improvement suggestions.

Visual Analytics

View your job search progress with beautiful charts and statistics. Track applications by status and identify patterns.

Smart Matching

Compare your resume against job descriptions to see how well you match and identify missing keywords.

CSV Export

Export all your applications to CSV format for backup or analysis in other tools.

Dark Mode

Easy on the eyes with automatic dark mode support. Work comfortably day or night.

Privacy & Security

Your data is important to us. Smart Job Tracker uses industry-standard security practices to protect your information:

  • Passwords are securely hashed using Bcrypt
  • All data is stored securely in MongoDB
  • Session management with secure cookies
  • No data is shared with third parties
